Simulation of the limitations of Human Auditory System

2nd Semester 2023-2024 | Human Computer Interaction | Archi Jain 2020B1A71380P

Two music samples will play simultaneously will different rhythms.

There are two simulations: one with a simple metronome to demonstrate the conflicting rhythm, and one with a full drum beat to show how this can be confusing in more complicated audios such as songs
Temporal conflict is introduced as the user finds these overlapping rhythms disorienting and distracting.
